Seleyna Velasquez

Student at SJSU | Majoring in Digital Media Art

Seleyna Velasquez is an artist based in San Jose, California. She is pursuing a Bachelors of Fine Arts in Digital Media Art at San Jose State University. Art has always been a big part of her life, growing up she was around a lot of creative people who fueled her creativity and passion for art. Her usual medium is digital illustration but she also likes to create with a number of different mediums. The meaning behind her artwork can vary, she likes to create art for pleasure or it can consist of a deeper meaning, based on emotion and nostalgia. She takes inspiration from things like tv shows, cartoons, family, and personal experiences.
