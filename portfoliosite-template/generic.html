Rainbow Falls

Rainbow Falls is a digital drawing featuring minimal 2D animated elements. It was created using Procreate, to draw the static landscape scene and animated assets, and After Effects, to animate the drawn assets. It was inspired by the style of retro travel posters, I find them to be visually appealing and I wanted to try to simulate that into this work. Rainbow Falls is a place I visited during a family trip to Hawaii in 2023. I remember this trip being full of fun times and feel grateful to experience it with my family. I have always enjoyed making work that connects to memories and using art as a tool to create a different form of a keepsake.
