Early Morning

Early Morning is a digital drawing featuring minimal 2D animated elements. The static elements of the piece and animated assets were drawn/animated in Procreate and After Effects. The sound that is incorporated in this piece is a genre called “lo-fi” which is considered relaxing background music that consists of ambient noise and static. This type of music is what I like to listen to when I’m focusing on a task and it often helps me relax, which inspired me to create my own lo-fi scene.
